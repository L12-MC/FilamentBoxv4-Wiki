Overview

Welcome to the FilamentBoxV4 wiki! This project is a 3D printed filament storage box designed to keep your filament organized.

Features

  • Ability to be sealed to a box with desiccant holder
  • Spool rollers compatible with various spool sizes
  • Smooth Rolling Spool Holders For Easier Spool Retraction
  • Compact and stackable design
  • Affordable and easy to print
  • Can be printed with any FDM printer
  • Can be printed in any color

FAQ

Q: What filament is recommended for printing?
A: PLA or PETG is recommended for durability and ease of printing.

Q: Can I fit larger spools?
A: The design supports up to 1kg spools, but modifications can be made for larger sizes.
